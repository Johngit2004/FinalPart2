About the Race

This marathon is a test of spirit and speed, open to all ages and fitness levels. To level the playing field, we've added timely obstacles that challenge fast-paced runners and give everyone a fair shot at victory. And to boost motivation beyond the prize, we may or may not release a few wild animals to gently encourage slower runners to pick up the pace.

Rules

  • Must be 25+ to register. We understand that peoples brain don't fully developed around this age and we want you to make sure this is not an impulsive decision.
  • No cheating or shortcuts! Cheaters will be dealt with harshly.
  • You must read and sign the online waiver. If anything happens out there from the obstacles placed by the event crew, we will not be responsible.
